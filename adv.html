Thrilling Adventures

Trek through lush Western Ghats, raft in roaring rivers, or discover hidden waterfalls.

Top Adventure Spots in Karnataka

  • Coorg (Kodagu)

    Trekking (Tadiandamol, Pushpagiri), Rafting (Barapole River), Waterfall rappelling, camping.

  • Chikmagalur

    Trekking (Mullayanagiri, Baba Budangiri), Coffee plantation trails, Ziplining.

  • Gokarna / Murudeshwar

    Beach trekking, Scuba diving, Snorkeling, Jet skiing, Parasailing.

  • Dandeli

    White-water rafting (Kali River), Kayaking, Canoeing, Jungle safaris.

  • Supa Dam Reservoir

    Kayaking, boating, and scenic water activities near Dandeli.

  • Anthargange

    Cave exploration, rock climbing, and night trekking.
