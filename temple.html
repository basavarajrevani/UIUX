Karnataka Heritage Roadmap

Virupaksha Temple, Hampi

This historic temple in Hampi is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is part of the Group of Monuments at Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Chennakeshava Temple, Belur

A masterpiece of Hoysala architecture, this temple is known for its intricate carvings and dedication to Lord Vishnu.

Hoysaleswara Temple, Halebidu

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is renowned for its elaborate sculptures and architectural elegance.

Pattadakal Monuments

A group of 7th and 8th-century monuments that blend northern and southern Indian architecture, also recognized as a UNESCO site.
