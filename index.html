Kadamba Dynasty (345 – 525 CE)

Region: Banavasi, North Karnataka

The earliest Kannada kingdom, laying the foundation for art, architecture, and administration. Banavasi was their capital, marking a significant era in Karnataka's history.

Chalukya & Hoysala Empires (6th - 14th CE)

Regions: Badami, Aihole, Pattadakal, Belur, Halebidu

Golden age of temple architecture. Sites like Badami, Aihole, Pattadakal (Chalukya) and Belur, Halebidu (Hoysala) showcase intricate craftsmanship that defines South Indian temple styles.

Vijayanagara Empire (1336 – 1646 CE)

Region: Hampi (Capital), Southern India

A powerful empire known for its grand capital Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Fostered a vibrant culture with significant contributions to arts, literature, and temple architecture.
